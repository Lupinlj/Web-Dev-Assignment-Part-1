From Home Kitchen to Your Heart

What started as weekend baking with his grandmother has blossomed into something beautiful. Joe Bakkersfield found his grandmother's recipe book after her passing and promised to turn her recipes into a legacy. After years of hard work, Joe Bakes was born, creating custom cakes, famous fudge, and beloved treats that bring smiles to our growing community.

  • Joe's famous homemade fudge
  • Custom cakes for birthdays and special events
  • Fresh brownies and cookies with amazing design
  • Affordable treats that put a smile on your face
  • Made fresh from our home kitchen in Cape Town
